Muffins, the early years

Hi. My name is Muffins. I am a mighty cat. I was adopted by my family when I was
only 3 weeks old.

My new mommy is a human. They are funny creatures.

I left the pound with my new mommy and new sister, Maria. They took me straight
to the pet store to buy:
  • A kitty baby bottle and formula

  • A small cloth toy

  • Litter box with litter

  • Food (for later)
 Mom and sis had to feed me by a bottle because I was so young.
